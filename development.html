Casa Grande — Why Here?

Equidistant from Phoenix and Tucson with large tracts of development-ready land, Casa Grande is uniquely positioned to host a major entertainment destination.

Site Strategy

  1. Secure 1,500–2,500 acres for phased development
  2. Anchor with theme park, two major resorts, and studio campus
  3. Create a transit spine linking to Phoenix/Tucson and I-10

Community & Incentives

Engage city leadership early. Propose jobs guarantees, local hiring targets, and infrastructure offsets. Seek tax incentives through Arizona Commerce and federal grants for rural development.
