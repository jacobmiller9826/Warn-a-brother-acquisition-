Legal Notes & IP

This is a conceptual project summary. IP rights and licensing must be negotiated with Warner Bros. Discovery or appropriate rights holders.

IP Strategy

Prefer a licensing / revenue-share model for use of Warner IP in theme park attractions and streaming reboots. Full acquisition scenarios would require extensive due diligence and regulatory review.

Content Policy

Adult-oriented reworks will enforce strict age-gating, parental controls, and clearly separated branding (WBD Black Label) from family-facing assets in the public park spaces.
