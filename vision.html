Executive Vision

Warner West leverages legacy Warner Bros. IP to build a vertically integrated entertainment destination in Casa Grande, Arizona.

Studio — WBD Black Label

R-rated reboots, auteur-driven features, and limited series that explore mature themes. Watchmen-style reinventions, dark animation, and prestige television aimed at adults.

  • Year 1: 3 high-profile reboots + 4 limited-series pilots
  • Year 2–3: 6–10 original productions per year
  • Festival partnerships & auteur talent development

Theme Park — Warner West

Multi-district immersive park: Noir City, Toon After Dark, Horror Haven, and Cine Village — each with adult-toned nighttime experiences and daytime accessibility.

  • Live theatrical spectacles, immersive LARP evenings, high-end dining and nightlife
  • Production tours & VR filmmaking labs in Cine Village
